# Auth Template

## Intent

Use this for login and other low-friction entry pages.

## Structure

- Brand or product intro
- Compact form area
- Helper links
- Copyright footer

## Notes

- Keep the surface calm and focused.
- The page should feel secure, direct, and readable.
- Use one visual accent only.

## Do

- Use `Card`, `Form`, `Input`, `Button`, `Link`, `Result`.

## Don’t

- Turn auth into a landing page.
- Over-style the background.
- Add unnecessary motion.
